Our Methodology

QazaqStep uses a micro-learning approach, breaking down complex grammar concepts into digestible lessons. Each lesson includes:

  • Grammar Explanation: Clear rules with practical examples
  • Mini Tests: 5-8 questions with instant feedback
  • Audio Dialogues: Real-world conversations for listening and shadowing practice
  • Vocabulary Cards: Spaced repetition system for word retention
  • Progress Tracking: Visual progress bars and achievement system
